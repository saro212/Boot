Hello, World!

What is art? Art is a visual object or experience consciously created through an expression of skill or imagination. The term art encompasses diverse media such as painting, sculputure, printmaking, drawing, decorative arts and many more. Art, a realm where creativity knows no bounds, encompasses various forms of expression. Let’s delve into types of art

1.Music

Melodies and harmonies transcend language barriers. Composers create symphonies, ballads, and jazz improvisations. Whether it’s a haunting violin solo or a foot-tapping pop song, music stirs our souls.

2.Literature

Words weave tales, evoke emotions, and ignite imaginations. Literature encompasses poetry, drama, novels, and essays. Writers craft narratives that resonate across time, capturing the essence of the human experience.

3.Architecture

Beyond aesthetics, architecture shapes our built environment. Architects blend creativity with functionality, designing structures that stand as cultural symbols. From soaring skyscrapers to serene temples, architecture influences our daily lives.
